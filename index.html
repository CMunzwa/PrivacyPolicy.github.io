We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share it with service providers or contractors only when necessary to deliver our services, and always under confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for service delivery or legal compliance.
